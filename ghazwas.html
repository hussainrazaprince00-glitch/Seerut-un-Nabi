The Important Battles

What is a Ghazwa?

• A battle led by the Prophet (PBUH) himself.
• There were 27 Ghazwas in total.
• They taught Muslims about unity and faith.

Ghazwa Name Year Main Detail
Badr 2 AH Muslims were 313 and won.
Uhud 3 AH Took place near Mount Uhud.
Khandaq 5 AH A trench was dug for safety.
Khaibar 7 AH Muslims marched to the forts.
Makkah 8 AH The conquest of the holy city.
