About Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) was born in the city of Makkah in the year 570. Even before he was a Prophet, people called him Al-Amin . When he was 40, he went to the Cave of Hira and got the first message from Allah. He told everyone that Allah is one and that we should always be kind to our neighbors and the poor.
